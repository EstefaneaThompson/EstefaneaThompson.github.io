My Story

Built from the ground up.

In 2015 I arrived in the United States from Brazil — no English, no degree, and no roadmap. What I had was a relentless work ethic and a belief that hard things are worth doing.

I spent years learning the language, studying, and finding my footing. In 2024 I graduated from Oklahoma State University with a B.S. in Management Information Systems — and I'm not stopping there. My Master's in Business Analytics & Data Science is on track for December 2026.

Today I work as a BI & Reporting professional at Williams Companies, a Fortune 500 energy infrastructure company — building the dashboards and data models that executives and operators depend on daily.

The hardship didn't define me. The choices I made because of it did.
