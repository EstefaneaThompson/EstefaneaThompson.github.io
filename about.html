About

A bit about me.

In 2015 I arrived in the United States from Brazil with no English, no degree, and no roadmap. What I had was a work ethic built from necessity and a belief that hard things are worth doing.

I spent years learning the language while working, studying while building a life from scratch. In 2024 I graduated from Oklahoma State University with a B.S. in Management Information Systems — the first in my family. I'm not stopping there. My Master's in Business Analytics & Data Science is on track for December 2026.

Today I work in IT Business Intelligence & Reporting at Williams Companies, a Fortune 500 energy infrastructure company. I build the Power BI dashboards and semantic data models that executives and operators depend on daily — translating sprawling enterprise data into clarity people can actually act on.

Before Williams, I interned in HR Analytics at CBIZ, designing onboarding frameworks and workforce analytics. Before America, I spent four years as a Business Analyst at Sintese Comercial Hospitalar in Goiânia — managing $300K+ in monthly receivables and automating finance reporting long before "automation" became a buzzword.

The hardship didn't define me. The choices I made because of it did.
